Jujutsu Kaisen

About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series that follows the adventures of Yuji Itadori as he joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to combat curses and protect humanity.

Yuji Itadori

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist, known for his incredible physical abilities and strong sense of justice. He becomes the vessel for a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna.

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi Fushiguro is a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High. He is skilled in summoning Shikigami and is one of the key allies of Yuji Itadori.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara Kugisaki is another first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High. She uses a variety of cursed tools and has a strong personality.

Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo is a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High and one of the most powerful sorcerers in the world. He is known for his laid-back personality and immense strength.
